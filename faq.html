Frequently Asked Questions

Where is Adventure Peak located?

Our facility is located in the Adirondack Mountains.

Is Adventure Peak a sleepaway camp?

Yes, Adventure Peak is a sleepaway camp. We ask that you please keep your child at camp for the entire duration of the program that they were signed up for.

What if my child needs to leave camp before the end of program?

Because of our heavy enrollment numbers, we ask that you only submit an enrollment form if you know your child will be available for the entire program. We generally do not accommodate requests for shorter program durations. In the event of an emergency, please contact our office at (xxx)xxx-xxxx.

What if my child gets homesick?

Surprisingly enough, we've found that children generally do not feel overwhelmingly homesick. While the occasional homesickness is fairly common, it is a completely normal and natural feeling. Our camp counselors are highly trained in child care and will connect with your child to ensure they feel at home. If you're worried about your child and want to stay in touch, we highly encourage sending letters to our very special Parent Inbox, located at our facility. We've found that staying in contact with your child has boosted morale and makes campers feel more at-ease knowing that their families are thinking about them!

What is your policy on technology?

We know that our campers staying in touch with their parents is an important aspect of growing up.
